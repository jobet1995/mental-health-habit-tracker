Common Questions

How do I track a habit?
Simply navigate to the "Habits" tab from the bottom menu, find your habit, and tap the checkmark circle to mark it as complete for today.
Can I edit my mood logs?
Yes! In the "Mood" tab, find your entry in the "Emotional History" section and tap the (i) info icon to view details, then select Edit.
What is Dark Mode?
Dark Mode reduces eye strain by using a darker color palette. You can toggle it on or off in the Settings menu.
